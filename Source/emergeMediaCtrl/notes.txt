emergeMediaCtrl
---------------

Functions:
* Showing the current song title in the middle region
* Pause/resume playing: click on the middle region
* Prev song: click on the left region
* Next song: click on the right region

Media Player suport
1. WinAmp: 
	- fully supported and tested

2. FooBar2000
	- fully supported with legacy code, but not tested

3. Media Player Classic
	- fully supported with legacy code, but not tested

4. Windows Media Player
	- No song title information
	- Pause/Prev/Next works in case of WMP9
	- Should work on newer releases, but not tested
	
	
Further development
* Support for WMP
* Internal commands for Pause/Prev/Next

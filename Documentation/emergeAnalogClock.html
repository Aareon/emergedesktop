About

emergeAnalogClock is an analog clock applet for Emerge Desktop.

Configuration

emergeAnalogClock settings are stored in HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Emerge Desktop\emergeAnalogClock and consist of the following subkeys:

  • Settings - all emergeAnalogClock related settings. Currently the options are:
    • X - The position of the window on the x-axis (Default 0)
    • Y - The position of the window on the y-axis (Default 0)
    • ActiveWindowAlpha - The degree of alpha transaprency of the window (Default 100) when the mouse if over the window
    • InactiveWindowAlpha - The degree of alpha transaprency of the window (Default 80) when the mouse if off the window
    • ZPosition - The position of the window on the z-axis (window order - Default "normal")
    • ShowSeconds - Display the 'seconds' hand (Default 0)
    • UseBackColor - Use the desktop background color as the background (Default 0)

Example configuration:

[HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Emerge Desktop\emergeAnalogClock]

[HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Emerge Desktop\emergeAnalogClock\Settings]
"x"=dword:00000000
"y"=dword:00000000
"zposition"="normal"
"activewindowalpha"=dword:00000064
"inactivewindowalpha"=dword:00000050
"showseconds"=dword:00000000
"usebackcolor"=dword:00000000

Usage

Double left clicking on emergeAnalogClock will bring up the 'Date and Time Properties' dialog.

Ctrl-Left clicking the window will allow for the moving of emergeAnalogClock around the desktop window. emergeAnalogClock will remember it's placement when moved. Holding down Shift while moving the window will be a temporary move, meaning emergeAnalogClock will not remember it's new placement.

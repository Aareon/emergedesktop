About

emergeDiskMon provides an on-screen disk activity light for a specified drive.

Configuration

emergeDiskMon settings are stored in HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Emerge Desktop\emergeDiskMon and consist of the following subkeys:

  • Settings - all emergeDiskMon related settings. Currently the options are:
    • X - The position of the window on the x-axis (Default 0)
    • Y - The position of the window on the y-axis (Default 0)
    • ActiveWindowAlpha - The degree of alpha transaprency of the window (Default 100) when the mouse if over the window
    • InactiveWindowAlpha - The degree of alpha transaprency of the window (Default 80) when the mouse if off the window
    • ZPosition - The position of the window on the z-axis (window order - Default "normal")
    • Monitor - The drive to monitor(Default "c:\")

Example configuration:

[HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Emerge Desktop\emergeDiskMon]

[HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Emerge Desktop\emergeDiskMon\Settings]
"X"=dword:00000338
"Y"=dword:000002e2
"ActiveWindowAlpha"=dword:00000050
"InactiveWindowAlpha"=dword:0000001e
"ZPosition"="Top"
"Monitor"="c:\\"

Usage

Ctrl-Left clicking the window will allow for the moving of emergeDiskMon around the desktop window. emergeDiskMon will remember it's placement when moved. Holding down Shift while moving the window will be a temporary move, meaning emergeDiskMon will not remember it's new placement.

About

emergeDigiClock is a digital clock applet for Emerge Desktop.

Configuration

emergeDigiClock settings are stored in HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Emerge Desktop\emergeDigiClock and consist of the following subkeys:

  • Settings - all emergeDigiClock related settings. Currently the options are:
    • X - The position of the window on the x-axis (Default 0)
    • Y - The position of the window on the y-axis (Default 0)
    • ActiveWindowAlpha - The degree of alpha transaprency of the window (Default 100) when the mouse if over the window
    • InactiveWindowAlpha - The degree of alpha transaprency of the window (Default 80) when the mouse if off the window
    • ZPosition - The position of the window on the z-axis (window order - Default "normal")
    • DateFormat - Date format, 0 for US, 1 for European (Default 0)

Example configuration:

[HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Emerge Desktop\emergeDigiClock]

[HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Emerge Desktop\emergeDigiClock\Settings]
"X"=dword:00000338
"Y"=dword:000002e2
"ActiveWindowAlpha"=dword:00000050
"InactiveWindowAlpha"=dword:0000001e
"ZPosition"="Top"
"DateFormat"=dword:00000001

Usage

Double left clicking on emergeDigiClock will bring up the 'Date and Time Properties' dialog.

Ctrl-Left clicking the window will allow for the moving of emergeDigiClock around the desktop window. emergeDigiClock will remember it's placement when moved. Holding down Shift while moving the window will be a temporary move, meaning emergeDigiClock will not remember it's new placement.
